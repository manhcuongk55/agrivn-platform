Frequently Asked Questions

Can I try Professional for free? ▼
Yes! Every Professional plan starts with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel anytime during the trial.
What payment methods do you accept? ▼
We accept Visa, Mastercard, bank wire transfer, and for Enterprise clients, custom invoicing with NET-30 terms. All payments are processed securely through Stripe.
How does Trade Finance work? ▼
Enterprise clients get access to pre-shipment financing, invoice factoring (up to 90 days), and Letter of Credit services through our banking partners. Apply directly from the dashboard after completing verification.
Can I switch plans anytime? ▼
Absolutely. Upgrade instantly at any time — you'll be charged a prorated amount. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing cycle. No cancellation fees.
What happens to my data if I cancel? ▼
Your data is retained for 90 days after cancellation. You can export all trade records, contacts, and analytics at any time. After 90 days, data is permanently deleted per our privacy policy.
Do you offer discounts for startups or NGOs? ▼
Yes! We offer 50% off Professional for verified startups (under 2 years old) and special pricing for agricultural NGOs and cooperatives. Contact us at partners@agrivn.com.
