📖 Our Story

Founded in 2012 by Mr. Nguyễn Văn Hùng and 46 neighboring farmers in Cư M'gar District, our cooperative manages 180 hectares of volcanic red basalt soil — among the best coffee-growing land in the world. We grow both Robusta and a small plot of Arabica at 800m elevation.

Every cherry is hand-picked at peak ripeness. We use a natural honey process on 40% of our crop, giving buyers distinctive sweet, fruity notes in the cup. Our wet-hulled Robusta is a staple for specialty roasters in Germany, South Korea, and Australia.

We don't sell to middlemen. Since joining AgriVN in 2024, we ship directly to roasters and importers worldwide — cutting 3 intermediaries and increasing farmer income by 28%.
