🔐 How We Build Trust for Every Farmer

Global buyers don't see the farm — we make sure they trust it anyway

🔬
Lab Testing
Every batch tested by SGS, Eurofins, or Bureau Veritas. Aflatoxin, pesticide, heavy metals. Results published on farmer profile.
📹
Farm Live Cam
Live video streams from harvest. Buyers can watch picking, weighing, and processing in real time. No middlemen, no mystery.
🗺️
GPS Farm Mapping
Every farm is GPS-mapped. Buyers see the exact plot, soil type, water source, and elevation on the farm's public profile.
🏅
Certification Hub
UTZ, GlobalGAP, USDA Organic, ISO 22000 — we help farmers get certified and display certs with expiry dates on every profile.
⭐
Buyer Reviews
Verified reviews from real importers after every shipment. 5-star ratings with detailed quality notes visible to all.
📱
QR Code Traceability
Every bag has a QR. Scan to see: which farm, which plot, which harvest date, which lab test. Consumer-facing transparency.
🔒
Escrow Payments
Buyers pay into escrow. Funds release when shipment is confirmed received and quality-checked. Zero risk for both sides.
📦
Sample Program
Buyers order 500g–2kg samples before committing to containers. Farmers get paid for samples. Builds confidence fast.
